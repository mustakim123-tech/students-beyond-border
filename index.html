About Us

At Students Beyond Bangladesh(SSB), we believe education is the foundation for a brighter future. We are a trusted education consultancy dedicated to guiding students toward the right academic and career paths, both locally and internationally. With a team of experienced counselors and global partners, we provide personalized support for every step of your journey — from choosing the right course and university, to preparing your application, securing your visa, and settling in a new country. Whether you dream of studying in Australia, Canada, the UK, the USA, or beyond, we’re here to help you succeed. What sets us apart is our commitment to integrity, transparency, and genuine care. We don’t just offer advice — we build futures.

Our Services Include:
  • University & Course Selection
  • Admission Application Support
  • Visa Guidance
  • Interview Preparation
  • Scholarship Assistance
  • Pre-departure Briefings
